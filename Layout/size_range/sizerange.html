Measurements should be taken directly on your body.


1 - CHEST

Measure your chest over the fullest part of your bust while wearing a bra that fits.


2 - BUST

If you're shopping for a bra you should take another measurement directly under the bust.


3 - WAIST

Your hip measurement should be measured around the fullest part of your hips.

Inner leg length is measured from the top of your inside leg down to the floor.

We have updated our ladies' sizes


After hearing customer feedback, we have updated our sizes to have a more suitable sleeve, torso length and leg inseam. We introduced these sizes as P sizes initially. However we will no longer label these as P on the products. These improved measurements will be our standard sizes.


For example:
S/P is S
M/P is M
34/P is 34

How to measure


Bust: Measure under your arms around the fullest part of your bust.

Waist: Measure at the natural crease of your waist.

Hips: Stand flat on the floor with your heels together. Measure around the fullest part of your

hips, approximately 8" below your waist.

Height: Stand with your back against the wall, with your feet slightly apart. Measure from the

floor to the top of your head.
